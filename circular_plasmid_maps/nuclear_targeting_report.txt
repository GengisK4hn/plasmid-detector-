SV40 NUCLEAR TARGETING SEQUENCE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

REFERENCES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SV40 nuclear localization signals (NLS) are among the most potent
nuclear targeting sequences known. They facilitate active transport
of DNA into the nucleus during interphase, affecting BOTH dividing
and non-dividing cells.

Key reference: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4150867/
Nuclear Localization of Plasmid DNA During Transfection

================================================================================
SIGNIFICANCE:
================================================================================
Plasmid DNA containing SV40 NLS can enter nuclei of non-dividing cells
through active nuclear transport mechanisms. This has implications
for genomic integration risk and long-term persistence.

CONCLUSION: Pfizer contains 4 more SV40
nuclear targeting elements than Moderna, representing higher
potential for nuclear uptake and genomic integration.
